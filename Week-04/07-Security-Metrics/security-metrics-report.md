\# Security Metrics \& Executive Reporting



\## Key SOC Metrics

\- Mean Time to Detect (MTTD): 2 hours

\- Mean Time to Respond (MTTR): 4 hours

\- False Positive Rate: Low due to threat intelligence validation



\## Executive Summary

During the review period, phishing-related alerts were detected and handled efficiently using centralized monitoring and automated response workflows. The SOC achieved an average detection time of 2 hours and a response time of 4 hours. Automated validation reduced false positives and improved analyst efficiency. Continued optimization of detection rules and automation is recommended to further strengthen SOC performance.
