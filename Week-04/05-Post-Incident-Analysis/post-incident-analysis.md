\## Root Cause Analysis (5 Whys)

1\. User clicked a phishing link.

2\. The email appeared legitimate.

3\. Email filtering was weak.

4\. Advanced phishing detection was not enabled.

5\. Security controls were not fully tuned.



\## SOC Metrics

\- Mean Time to Detect (MTTD): 2 hours

\- Mean Time to Respond (MTTR): 4 hours



\## Lessons Learned

The incident emphasized the need for stronger email security controls and user awareness training. Automating phishing response reduced response time and analyst workload. Continuous monitoring and periodic improvement of SOC processes are recommended.
