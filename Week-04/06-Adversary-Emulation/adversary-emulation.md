\# Adversary Emulation – Phishing (T1566)



\## Objective

Simulate a phishing attack to validate SOC detection capability.



\## Technique

MITRE ATT\&CK T1566 – Phishing.



\## Emulation Scenario

1\. A phishing email with a malicious link is simulated.

2\. User interacts with the link.

3\. Endpoint activity is generated.



\## Expected Detection

Wazuh ingests Windows authentication and application logs, making events visible to SOC analysts.



\## Outcome

This validates alert visibility and analyst readiness against phishing attacks.
