Executive Summary:
A simulated phishing-based security incident was detected and handled
by the Security Operations Center (SOC). The incident involved suspicious
file download activity originating from an external source. Prompt
detection and automated response mechanisms prevented further impact.

Incident Timeline:
- Initial Activity Detected: Phishing alert generated by Wazuh SIEM
- Alert Triage: High-priority alert reviewed and validated
- Response Action: Malicious IP blocked and incident case created
- Containment: Endpoint communication restricted
- Closure: Incident documented and reviewed

The SOC detected the incident through proactive monitoring and alert
triage processes. The alert was classified as high priority due to the
potential risk of malware execution.

Response and Containment:
Automated SOAR playbooks were used to validate indicators, block
malicious IP addresses, and create an incident case for investigation.

- Mean Time to Detect (MTTD): 2 hours
- Mean Time to Respond (MTTR): 4 hours
- Dwell Time: 6 hours
